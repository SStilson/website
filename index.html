About Me

Hi, I'm Samantha. I graduated from Harvey Mudd College this past May with a BS in International Relations and a minor in Computer Science - a combination that's left me with an understanding of the global reach of technology, serious communication skills, the ability to create a fully functioning website in hours, and an analytical mindset that can switch from empathy to data effortlessly. So far, I've used my skills to design and code some cool products at brand new startups in education and home improvement. I've also managed social media policy and designed SEO strategies, and I'm currently in the process of launching a personal blog with a Wordpress theme I built from scratch.

When not working, I love exploring, talking foreign policy, trying new recipes, and writing. I studied abroad twice while in university and I'm always planning my next adventure. As you can see below, learning is my favorite thing to do and I'm always grateful for book, podcast, and online course recommendations.

Code is changing the way the world works, and I want to make sure this change is driven by the needs of real people and is solving real problems. In order to make this contribution, I am currently looking for full-time opportunities in product marketing and management at tech companies making a real difference. Thank you for visiting.
