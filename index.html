About me.

Hi, I'm Samantha. I'm a senior at Harvey Mudd College, majoring in International Relations and minoring in Computer Science. I have studied abroad twice (in Sydney and London) and in my free time you can usually find me planning my next international adventure. When I'm not wanderlusting, I can usually be found with my nose nearly pressed into my computer screen, squinting at some code and trying to make sure all the semicolons are in place. I think code is changing the way that the world works, and I want to be a part of that change. Thank you for visiting.
