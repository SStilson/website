About Me

Hi, I'm Samantha. I'm a recent grad of Harvey Mudd College, currently interning at early-stage startup Beyrep doing front-end engineering, design, and product management. I can usually be found wireframing new design ideas, checking in with our partner team in India, or playing with the latest JavaScript frameworks.

When not working, I love hiking the many trails of Southern California, reading about the foreign policy of nations across the world, and writing. I studied abroad twice while in university and I'm always planning my next adventure.

Code is changing the way the world works, and I want to make sure this change is driven by the needs of real people and the desire to solve real problems. In order to make this contribution, I am currently looking for full-time opportunities in user experience, front-end/mobile engineering, or product management. Thank you for visiting.
